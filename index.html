FAQ

Straight answers. No hype.

Do I need new software or a new CRM?

No. We can start with what you already use (Gmail + Google Sheets), or set up a simple CRM if needed.

Will this reply to customers automatically?

Yes, an instant acknowledgement can be automated. You can keep full control over final quotes and pricing.

How do you handle GDPR and data?

We only collect what you already collect for quoting, and keep the data in your tools where possible.

Can I change the messages later?

Yes. You’ll have editable templates and a simple process to update them anytime.

What if I already have lots of work booked?

Then we can prioritise organisation and pipeline visibility, or pause follow-ups during busy periods.
